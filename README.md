GiantDwarf Game App

this app enables multiple users to signIn and play.
the game steps are:

1. Sign Up
2. Enter your personal details.
3. logIn into the game
4. Create group or enter existing group.
5. Submit and Play.
6. The App will send you, your GIANT and his information.
